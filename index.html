What is CSI ?

Computer Society of India is a nonprofit professional body formed to exchange views and information and to learn and share ideas. CSI was first formed in the year 1965 by a handful of IT enthusiasts who felt the need to organize their activities. It is the first, largest, and the most professionally managed association of and for IT professionals in India. Currently having 488 student branches and rooted firmly in 73 different locations, CSI is planning to expand its horizons to other cities and countries with an objective to provide more opportunities and contribute more.

Why join CSI ?

CSI SCT SB aims to develop the technical skills of students for their professional development by providing numerous webinars and workshops on leading topics. A great platform to showcase your technical skillsets and knowledge. Moreover, you can be a part of a network with dedicated academicians, scientists, and alumni.

What we do ?

CSI SCT SB hosts various events including lecture meetings, seminars, conferences, training programs, programming/coding contests, and practical visits to installations. This is also a very vast space to exchange views and information, learn and share ideas. The society does everything possible to bring out the best skills in its members.
